ABOUT THE COMPANY

Built On Paper,
Backed By Record.

Every claim on this page is backed by a certificate of incorporation and a live status report on the Corporate Affairs Commission register.

Strengthco Construction Services Limited is incorporated under the Companies and Allied Matters Act 2020 and licensed to carry on the business of real estate development, acquisition, leasing, letting, sale and management of land, buildings and other landed properties.

We operate projects nationwide with our head office in Kabaki Plaza, Jahi, Abuja. Every project is structured around three things: verifiable title, disciplined construction, and excellent client experience.

The company is privately held by its two founding directors in equal measure.

WHAT WE DO

Six Ways We
Move A Project.

From the first land search to the last mortgage disbursement, each service exists to remove one specific risk from a property transaction.

01 / LAND

Acquisition & Development

Land search, title verification and ground-up development of residential, commercial and mixed-use schemes across Nigeria.

02 / BUILD

Construction Management

On-site supervision from foundation to finishing, coordinating contractors, materials and inspection against approved drawings.

03 / OWN

Sale & Subscription

Structured payment plans and a subscriber register that tracks every unit from reservation through to title transfer.

04 / FUND

Mortgage Facilitation

Direct introductions to commercial banks and primary mortgage institutions, with documentation support through to approval.

05 / LEASE

Leasing & Letting

Tenant sourcing and lease administration for completed commercial and residential units within our portfolio.

06 / HOLD

Property Management

Ongoing facility management, maintenance coordination and investment oversight for owners who hold rather than flip.
